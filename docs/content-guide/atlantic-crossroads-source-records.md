# Case 1.01 Source Records

## 1. The Caribbean—Island Society

- Type: Secondary context
- Creator: Library of Congress exhibition text
- Purpose in game: Establish pre-contact Caribbean society and prevent the case from treating European arrival as the beginning of history.
- Source: Library of Congress, _1492: An Ongoing Voyage_.

## 2. Letter Reporting on the First Voyage

- Type: Primary source letter
- Creator: Christopher Columbus
- Date: 1493
- Purpose in game: Practice sourcing for point of view, audience, and European expedition goals.
- Source: Library of Congress facsimile and English translation.

## 3. Universalis cosmographia

- Type: Primary source map
- Creator: Martin Waldseemüller
- Date: 1507
- Purpose in game: Practice reading maps as evidence of European geographic knowledge and assumptions. It is not a direct depiction of Caribbean life in 1493.
- Source: Library of Congress Geography and Map Division.
