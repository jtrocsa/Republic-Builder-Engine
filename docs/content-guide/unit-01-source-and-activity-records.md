# Unit 1 Source and Activity Records

## Source display policy

- All student-facing source text and visual assets must load from the repository.
- External catalog links are reference links only; they must not be required to complete an activity.
- Interpretive visuals are labeled as interpretive; they are never presented as primary sources.
- Secondary context is clearly labeled and is revealed after a student's initial response when used as feedback.

## APUSH alignment

Unit 1 covers Indigenous societies before European contact, European exploration, the Columbian Exchange, labor/slavery/caste in the Spanish colonial system, and cultural interactions among Europeans, Native Americans, and Africans.
