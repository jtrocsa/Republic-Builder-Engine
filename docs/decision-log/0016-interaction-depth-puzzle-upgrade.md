# Decision 0016 — Interaction Depth, Puzzle Upgrade, and Field Physics

Milestone 3.4.3 keeps the improved Caribbean world but deepens the evidence interactions and repairs movement/collision clarity.

## Changes

- Standardizes field NPC proportions so Taíno and Spanish characters match the Chronicler scale.
- Updates the field physics layer so collision is based on the player’s feet, not full sprite center.
- Splits decorative art from walkable space: building bases, ship hulls, canoes, tents, palms, crates, and NPCs block movement.
- Reduces per-step movement distance and timing for smoother travel.
- Rebuilds the village investigation as a short field-observation sequence rather than a single Observe button.
- Reworks the Columbus encounter with dramatized but historically grounded dialogue before the POV checkpoint.
- Replaces the four-quadrant Waldseemüller activity with a ten-piece reconstruction puzzle.
