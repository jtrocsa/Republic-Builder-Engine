# Decision 0013 — Playable Chronicle Institute Hub

The Chronicle Institute is a present-day, playable top-down home base rather than a menu. The foyer and Archive room are one physical building. Students can talk with the Director and researchers, then approach the physical Navigation Table to open the geographic route map.

Historical settings do not contain a visible Institute Archive building. The Codex is the portable link between field investigation and the present-day Archive.

## Current interaction loop

1. Walk inside the Institute.
2. Speak with recurring Institute staff or approach the Navigation Table.
3. Open the Atlantic route map from the Table.
4. Initiate chronotravel to a teacher-unlocked case.
5. Submit the field record through the Codex.
6. Return physically to the Institute after the Archive receives the transmission.
