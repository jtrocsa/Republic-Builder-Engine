# Decision 0011 — Unit 1 Campaign Loop and Signature Activities

Unit 1 uses three cases, each with a distinct mechanic attached to historical thinking:

- Record Reconstruction: sorts evidence by the historical question it can answer.
- Exchange Ledger: links movement across the Atlantic to groups affected and historical consequences.
- Colonial System Builder: tests a causal explanation of Spanish colonial systems.

The mechanics intentionally avoid trivia/reward loops. Each requires evidence interpretation and leads into a written response or AP-style assessment task.
