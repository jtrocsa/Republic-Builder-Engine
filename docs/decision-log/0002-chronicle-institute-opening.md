# Decision 0002 — Chronicle Institute Opening Sequence

**Status:** Accepted

## Decision

Chronicle begins at the Chronicle Institute in the present day. The player is recruited as a Chronicler, receives the premise of historical investigation, and begins character creation before entering the first historical setting.

## Milestone 1 scope

Milestone 1 implements only the opening/orientation flow:

1. Chronicle title screen
2. Chronicle Institute premise
3. Field protocol / Chronicler orientation
4. Character-creation handoff placeholder
5. First assignment preview: Unit 1, Case 1.01 — _The Atlantic Crossroads_

## Deliberate exclusions

This milestone does not implement avatar choices, saving, time travel, maps, quests, sources, assessments, or teacher features.

## Rationale

The opening gives every later feature a clear narrative purpose. Students are not merely opening an APUSH tool; they are becoming Chroniclers who investigate evidence and submit reports to the Institute.
