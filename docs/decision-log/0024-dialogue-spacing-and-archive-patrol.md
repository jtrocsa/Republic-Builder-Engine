# 0024 — Dialogue spacing and Archive patrol

The field dialogue system was revised so NPC speech is anchored to the speaker instead of appearing as a fixed panel at the screen edge. This reduces perceived camera snap, removes stale saved dialogue after reload, and keeps the field readable when several interactable labels are nearby.

Caribbean field markers were spread out: the village observation sits nearer the village center, the Columbus source sits nearer Columbus, the cartographer table/puzzle station moved away from the ship source marker, and the recall beacon moved farther from the puzzle station.

The Institute Archive now uses gentle NPC patrols for Director Hale, Dr. Soto, and Professor Park. The Preservation Case was moved to the upper bookshelf area so it reads as a shelf object rather than a floating status label.
